About Me

Kai Keefe is a filmmaker, videographer & editor from the DMV area. He is passionate about telling stories that highlight the extraordinary in the ordinary. As a freelancer, Keefe has done numerous independent projects as well as contract work for clients such as The Newseum, the Women’s March, the American Bar Association, and 522 Productions. He has also received praise for his prolific creative work, which includes narrative short films and music made using everyday household objects and sounds as instruments.

Keefe is especially interested in telling stories about environmental issues, women’s rights, gender equity, and income inequality.
